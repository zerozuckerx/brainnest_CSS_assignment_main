My guitar journey

I started playing the guitar about 6 years ago. A friend of mine always had his electrical guitar standing in the living room and some day I wanted to try and play something. It was the riff of the Artic Monkeys song "Do I Wanna Know". A few weeks later I bought my very first electrical guitar and never looked back since.

A few years ago I was really into playing the electric guitar, playing some heavy riffs or learning my favorite solos, usually from Black Sabbath or similar bands. Nowadays I prefer playing my western guitar, strumming or playing fingerstyle songs while singing to them.

Why the guitar is arguably the best instrument:

  • versatile
  • small and transportable
  • lots of different sounds possible
